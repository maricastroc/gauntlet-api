Concept mock · Bracket

Operate a tournament the way you commentate one.

Three high-fidelity screens of the tournament operation tool. The heart of the product isn't the aesthetics — it's the always-correct derived state: standings, tiebreak criteria and bracket advancement that recalculate, in a transaction, with every result entered. These screens show how that appears to whoever operates it.

About the mock. Fictional data; real teams only to add texture. The thesis of these screens is that the engineering value isn't in the table itself, but in keeping it always coherent — the scenario in screen 03 (editing an already-entered result and watching the effect propagate through tiebreak and bracket, in a transaction) is exactly what the backend has to guarantee under concurrency.
